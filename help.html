Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center! Here are some common questions and their answers:

How do I add an expense?

To add an expense, navigate to the expense tracker page, enter the date, item, amount, and category, and click "Add Expense."

How do I download my expense report?

You can download your expense report by clicking the "Download PDF" button on the expense tracker page.

What should I do if I have a problem?

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@expensetracker.com.
